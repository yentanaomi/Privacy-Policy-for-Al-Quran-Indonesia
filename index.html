Privacy Policy for Al-Quran Indonesia

Last updated: 21 February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Al-Quran Indonesia ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with your use of our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

We prioritize your privacy and minimal data collection. We only access permissions and data necessary for the App's core functionality.

A. Location Data (Approximate and Precise)

The App requests access to your device's location (ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION and ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION).

  • Purpose:
    • Prayer Times (Jadwal Sholat): To calculate accurate prayer times based on your specific geographic coordinates.
    • Nearby Mosques (Masjid Terdekat): To identify and display mosques in your vicinity using the Overpass API (OpenStreetMap data).
    • Location Name: To display your current City and District name on the home screen using Google's Geocoding service.
  • Data Handling: Your location data is processed ephemerally and is not stored on our servers. Coordinates are sent to third-party services solely to retrieve necessary data.

B. User Provided Information

  • User Name: You have the option to enter your name for a personalized greeting. This name is stored locally on your device (in SharedPreferences) and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

C. Device and Usage Data

We and our third-party partners (like Google AdMob) may collect certain device information:

  • Identifiers: Advertising ID (AAID) and device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Crash logs, diagnostics, and app performance data.
  • Purpose: To serve personalized advertisements, analyze app performance, and prevent fraud.

2. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions for specific features:

  • Location: Required for Prayer Times and Nearby Mosques.
  • Internet: Required to fetch Quran data, prayer times, and load advertisements.
  • Network State: To check internet connectivity before making API calls.
  • Notifications: To show audio playback controls in the notification bar.
  • Wake Lock: To keep the CPU running while playing Quran audio in the background.
  • Foreground Service: To ensure Quran audio continues playing when you minimize the App.

3. Third-Party Services and APIs

These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements.
  • Equran.id API: To fetch the list of Surahs and Verses.
  • OpenStreetMap / Overpass API: To find and display nearby mosques.
  • Google Maps & Geocoding: To display navigation and convert coordinates into City/District names.

4. Data Safety

  • No Personal Account Required: You can use the App instantly without creating an account.
  • No Server Storage: We do not operate a backend server that stores your personal messages or location history.
  • Encryption: All data transmission occurs over secure HTTPS connections.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our App is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
